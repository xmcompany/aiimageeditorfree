## Introduction

Welcome to AI Video Generator Free, an AI video and image creation platform at [aivideogeneratorfree.org](https://aivideogeneratorfree.org). ## Information Collection and Use

We collect information to keep your AI Video Generator Free experience fast, secure, and personalized:

1. **Account Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Name, email address, password, and profile preferences you submit when you sign up.
   - **Purpose**: To authenticate you, deliver account features, and provide customer support.

2. **Usage Details**
   - **What We Collect**: Logs of prompts, feature interactions (e.g., text-to-video, image-to-video, model selection), and timestamps.
   - **Purpose**: To improve model quality, personalize recommendations, and ensure platform reliability.

3. **Device Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and approximate location derived from IP address.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize the interface for your device, troubleshoot bugs, and prevent abuse.

4. **Cookies**
   - **What We Collect**: Session tokens, analytics identifiers, and preference cookies.
   - **Purpose**: To keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and measure performance.

5. **Payment and Billing Information**
   - **What We Collect**: Billing name, address, VAT or tax identifiers, and limited card details processed by our payment partner.
   - **Purpose**: To process purchases of AI Video Generator Free credits, subscriptions, or add-on features.

6. **Generated Content**
   - **What We Collect**: Input prompts, uploaded reference images, and generated outputs.
   - **Purpose**: To deliver requested generations, provide history for your account, and improve safety systems.

## Data Storage and Security

We store your information on secure cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and ongoing monitoring. While we strive for industry-leading protections, no transmission or storage method is completely secure. Please use strong passwords and notify us immediately of any suspected unauthorized access.
